Indian Fruits

Categories of Indian Fruits

Indian fruits can be categorized into various groups based on their characteristics and botanical classifications. Some common categories include tropical fruits like mango, banana, and guava, citrus fruits such as oranges and lemons, and stone fruits like peaches and plums. Each category offers a unique array of flavors and nutritional benefits, making Indian fruits a delightful and healthy addition to any diet.
